## CINEMATIC / FILM

### 1. Post-Apocalyptic Survivor
Wind whips sand across the ruins of a collapsed city. A 28-year-old female survivor stands on a crumbling structure. Her torn gray cape billows in the wind; her face is covered in dust and old scars. At first she tilts her head against the gust. After a brief pause, something shifts on the horizon. In the distance, the blurred silhouette of a mechanical creature moves slowly. She raises her gaze. Her breathing steadies. Her eyes shift from confusion to resolve. The camera pushes from a medium shot to a close-up, guided by her line of sight - as if the world is coming back into focus. As the camera advances, the sandstorm softens and light breaks through the dust. Post-apocalyptic sci-fi style, desaturated earth tones. Realistic wind physics, light diffusion, and fabric movement.

### 2. Retired Soldier in the Rain
Heavy rain hammers the roof of an abandoned factory. A hard-faced man in his early 30s stands at the edge. His worn dark-gray military coat clings to his soaked frame. A faint scar on his right cheek flickers under the distant glow of a neon sign. He looks down, lighting his last cigarette with a slightly trembling hand. A two-second pause. The smoke barely rises before the rain scatters it. In the distance, neon reflections shimmer in the puddles. He slowly raises his head - his expression shifting from exhaustion to cold resolve. The camera drifts slowly from a rear-side shot to a frontal medium shot, as if pulled by an invisible force. As it moves in, water streams down his face and his breath becomes visible. Dark realism style, high-contrast cool blue palette, cinematic film texture. Realistic rain physics, wet fabric clinging to the body, smoke dispersed by wind.

### 3. Cyberpunk Assassin Rooftop

During the rotation, neon reflections glide across wet metal and her suit's surface. The motion stops at a low frontal angle. The eye's glow intensifies. Cyberpunk aesthetic, high-contrast purple and red neon. Realistic rain reflections on metal, natural movement of wet fabric.

## CHARACTER SHEET + ANTI-BLOCCO (BYPASS FILTER)

> Workflow di @vladimircherner per creare character sheet che bypassano il filtro di Seedance 2.0.
> Seedance interpreta il character sheet come un 3D render invece che una foto live, aumentando le chance di passare.

### 76. Character Sheet Prompt Structure (@vladimircherner)

Usa questo prompt in un generatore (Midjourney, Flux, etc.) per creare il character sheet da usare poi come reference in Seedance:

```
{
  "TEXT_ELEMENTS": {
    "visible_text": "HEAD DETAILS, FULL BODY ORTHOGRAPHIC GRIDS, ADDITIONAL FEATURES, MATERIAL TEXTURES, FRONT, PROFILE, LEFT 45°, SIDE, BACK, HEAD AND SHOULDERS, EYES, FEET SHOES, [UPPER GARMENT NAME], HAIR, SKIN TEXTURE, [FOOTWEAR/ACCESSORY NAME]",
    "typography": "small bold black sans serif, compact size, unobtrusive scaling",
    "placement": "headers aligned top left of each respective layout block, small sub labels centered directly beneath individual angles and macro textures"
  },
  "SHOT": {
    "composition": "structured multi panel character reference sheet, clean grid arrangement",
    "detailed_focus": "razor sharp center, edge to edge clarity across all sub panels, infinite depth of field, no background blur",
    "blocking": "four distinct structural blocks top left for portraits, top right for full height turnarounds, bottom left for additional features, bottom right for material crops",
    "framing": "symmetrical layout, precise anatomical alignment across views, uniform subject scaling"
  },
  "grid_block_1_head_details": "close up framing, eye level angle. Includes three panels direct front view, profile side view, left 45 degree angle view",
  "grid_block_2_full_body": "full body framing, eye level angle. Includes four panels direct front view, left 45 degree angle view, side profile view, and a complete head to toe rear view",
  "grid_block_3_additional_features": "mixed framing and angles. Includes three distinct panels: 1. Top down overhead bird eye perspective, showing the top of the character's head and shoulders and visible footwear. 2. Macro eye close up capturing detailed iris color and pupil. 3. Close up on feet and [describe subject's specific footwear]",
  "grid_block_4_materials": "macro view framing, extreme close up. Includes four square panels detailing [describe upper garment fabric/texture], natural hair texture with [specific hair details], natural skin texture with [specific skin details, e.g., light freckles/pores], and [describe footwear or accessory texture]"
}
```

**NOTE:** Il prompt NON specifica stile visivo, background, cinematografia o parametri di framing - quelli li decidi tu. Sostituisci le parti tra [parentesi quadre] con la descrizione del tuo personaggio.

### Workflow Completo Anti-Blocco:

**Step 1:** Genera il character sheet con il prompt sopra (Midjourney, Flux, Nano Banana, etc.)
- Seedance lo interpreta come 3D render, non come foto reale = piu chance di passare il filtro

**Step 2:** Se viene ancora bloccato, prova queste alternative:
- **Crop del viso:** Ritaglia solo il primo piano del volto e caricalo come seconda reference
- **Grid overlay:** Sovrapponi una griglia sull'immagine in Photoshop
- **Aggiungi noise:** Aggiungi rumore/grana all'immagine
- **Converti in vettoriale:** Trasforma la foto in immagine vettoriale
- **Sperimenta:** I risultati variano, la chiave e' provare metodi diversi se la foto viene rifiutata

### 78. Anchor-First Prompt per Consistency (da usare CON il character sheet)

Ogni prompt Seedance deve avere 2 blocchi:
```
BLOCCO 1 - Character Anchor (FISSO, mai cambiare):
Character: [nome], [genere], [tono pelle], [capelli], [segni distintivi],
[mano dominante], [espressione tipica].
Keep these features unchanged.
Maintain facial features and wardrobe fully consistent with @Image1.
Character from @Image1 must stay identical. Same face, hair, outfit.
No face changes, no flicker.

BLOCCO 2 - Scena (VARIABILE, cambia per shot):
Shot [N]: [tipo inquadratura], [azione], [ambientazione], [luce],
[movimento camera], [audio/ambiente].
```
