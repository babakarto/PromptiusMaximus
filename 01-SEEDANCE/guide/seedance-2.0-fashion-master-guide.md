## Perche Seedance 2.0 e il Migliore per Fashion Video

- **Fisica dei tessuti realistica** (drape, shimmer, gravity, wind interaction)
- **Coerenza outfit perfetta** con @Image1 (75-85% weight)
- **Camera cinematografica editoriale** (hip-height tracking, orbiting, low-angle)

## 1. Regole Avanzate per Fashion Prompts (Testate)

### Master Template (Copia-Incolla e Adatta)

```
@Image1 (75-85% weight) exact outfit / fabric / garment reference.
Model wearing [descrizione precisa del capo + materiali].
Fabric flows naturally with realistic physics, drape, shimmer, gravity and wind response.

[00-04s] Shot 1 + camera + action.
[04-08s] Shot 2 + turn/pose.
[08-12s] Shot 3 + highlight o climax.

Camera: [hip-height tracking / low-angle / 360 orbit / slow push-in].
Lighting: [soft diffused studio / dramatic side lighting / golden hour / neon].
Style: [Zara campaign / high-fashion editorial / luxury runway / commercial].
Ultra-smooth 60fps, perfect garment coherence, cinematic color grading, 2K resolution.

Constraints: realistic fabric simulation, natural gravity and drape, no jitter, no deformation, consistent lighting.
```

### Vocabolario Tessuti & Fisica (Termini Esatti)

| Tessuto | Frasi Esatte |
|---------|-------------|
| **Silk** | flowing silk, natural inertia, sheen under lights, elegant curve when turning |
| **Wool / Cashmere** | oversized wool coat, soft drape, gentle fabric movement |
| **Denim / Leather** | structured denim, leather shine, realistic creases |
| **Tulle / Organza** | billowing tulle, layered movement |
| **Physics (sempre)** | realistic fabric simulation, wind-induced movement, water droplets sliding off, motion blur on fast fabric |

### Camera Fashion-Specific (Le Piu Efficaci)

- **Hip-height tracking shot** (mostra meglio il drape)
- **Low-angle tracking** following boots/heels
- **Slow 360 orbital shot** (per macro garment)
- **Side-angle + gradual orbit**

# 12. Beauty Video Prompts & Ideas for Seedance 2.0

## 12.1 Core Beauty Prompt Rules (Tested Best Practices)

- **Lead with product + action:** specifica sempre "realistic skin texture", "dewy glow", "viscous serum drip"
- **Use @Image1 for product/hand lock** (70-85% weight) → coerenza perfetta del packaging e del gesto
- **Camera = macro + slow push-in** → classic beauty editorial feel
- **Timeline format** → perfetto per tutorial step-by-step o UGC ads
- **Physics line (obbligatoria):** `"realistic liquid viscosity, skin absorption glow, natural hand movement, subtle bubbles and reflections"`

### Master Template for Any Beauty Video (Copia-Incolla)

```
@Image1 (75-85% weight) exact product / hand / skin reference.
Close-up of [product name] on flawless dewy skin.
[Action: applying / spreading / blending] with realistic texture and glow.

[00-04s] Shot 1 + macro camera + initial application.
[04-08s] Shot 2 + blending / transformation.
[08-12s] Shot 3 + final glow / reveal.

Camera: extreme macro / slow push-in / orbiting shot, 85mm lens, shallow depth of field.
Lighting: soft ring light / dramatic beauty lighting / cool-toned studio.
Style: luxury skincare commercial / ASMR tutorial / UGC lipstick ad.
Ultra-smooth 60fps, perfect product coherence, cinematic color grading, realistic skin physics.
```

### Vocabolario Beauty-Specific (Termini Esatti)

| Categoria | Frasi Esatte |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Skin** | dewy glow, flawless porcelain skin, subtle subsurface scattering, visible pores closing |
| **Product** | viscous serum dripping, gel with tiny bubbles, creamy texture melting on skin |
| **Motion** | gentle circular blending, fingertip tapping, brush sweeping through hair |
| **Camera** | 85mm macro lens, rack focus from product to skin, slow orbiting around bottle |
