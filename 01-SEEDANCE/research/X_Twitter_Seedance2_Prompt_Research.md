### 6.6 General Tips Aggregated from X Threads

**Prompt Structure (confirmed by multiple creators):**
```
Subject + Action + Camera + Scene + Style + Constraints
```

**Optimal Length:**
- 30-100 words for best results (official)
- 120-280 words for detailed multi-shot (SeaArt guide)
- One-liners work surprisingly well for simple concepts

**Key Keywords That Improve Quality:**
- "cinematic" or "4K" at the end
- "slow motion" for smoother movement
- Film director references (Denis Villeneuve, Wong Kar-wai, Christopher Nolan)
- Specific camera terminology (dolly zoom, rack focus, POV tracking shot, whip pan)

**What NOT To Do:**
- No negative prompts (not supported) - describe what you WANT instead
- Don't use vague words like "cool" or "nice lighting"
- Don't overload with more than 2 characters (identity confusion)
- One shot = one verb

**Asset References:**
- @Image1, @Image2, @Image3 (up to 9 images)
- @Video1, @Video2, @Video3 (up to 3 videos, 15s each)
- @Audio1, @Audio2, @Audio3 (up to 3 audio, 15s each)

**Multi-Shot Timeline Format:**
```
Shot 1 [00-05s]: Description. Camera. Sound.
Shot 2 [05-10s]: Description. Camera. Sound.
Shot 3 [10-15s]: Description. Camera. Sound.
```

**Censorship/Nerfing Warning (from @javilopen):**
- Model is progressively being restricted
- Words like "gun" can block generation
- Real celebrity likenesses being restricted
- "By the time they open launch it, it will be much more limited"
