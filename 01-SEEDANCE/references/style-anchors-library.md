# Style Anchors Library -- Seedance 2.0 Reference

> Style anchors are single references that tell the AI model exactly what visual style to aim for.
> "Blade Runner 2049 cinematography" produces better results than stacking "moody, cinematic, dramatic, dark, atmospheric".
> Use ONE strong anchor per prompt. Combine only with a film stock or post-processing modifier when needed.

## How to Combine (Rules)

### Combination Priority Order
```
[Camera Angle] + [Movement] + [Subject/Action] + [Style Anchor] + [Film Stock] + [Quality Suffix]
```

### Rules

1. **One style anchor maximum** -- never stack "Wes Anderson + Kubrick + Blade Runner." Pick one.
2. **Style anchor goes AFTER camera/subject, BEFORE quality suffix.**
3. **Film stock can combine with a style anchor** -- they operate on different axes.
   - OK: `Wes Anderson symmetrical composition, Kodak Portra 400 warmth`
   - OK: `Film noir, Kodak Tri-X 400 black and white`
4. **Post-processing anchors stack with anything** -- they modify the final image.
   - OK: `Blade Runner 2049 cinematography, heavy film grain, subtle vignette`
5. **Never combine two directors.** Choose the dominant visual identity.
6. **Brand anchors replace director anchors** -- do not combine Apple + Wes Anderson.
7. **Render engine anchors are for CG/3D only** -- do not pair with film stocks.
8. **Genre anchors can substitute for director anchors** when no specific director reference fits.

### Valid Combination Examples

| Combo | Prompt Fragment |
|---|---|
| Director + Film Stock | `...Wes Anderson symmetrical composition, shot on Kodak Portra 400` |
| Director + Post-Processing | `...David Fincher Se7en visual style, desaturated bleach bypass` |
| Genre + Film Stock + Post | `...film noir, Kodak Tri-X 400 black and white, heavy film grain, subtle vignette` |
| Brand + Quality | `...Apple product launch style, shot on RED V-RAPTOR 8K` |
| Animation + Post | `...Spider-Verse halftone animation, chromatic aberration` |

### Invalid Combinations (Avoid)

| Bad Combo | Why |
|---|---|
| `Wes Anderson + Stanley Kubrick` | Conflicting symmetry philosophies; pick one |
| `Apple style + Kodachrome` | Brand precision clashes with analog texture |
| `Unreal Engine 5 + Kodak Portra 400` | CG render + analog film stock = contradictory signal |
| `Pixar + Arcane` | Two animation styles; the model will average them into mush |
