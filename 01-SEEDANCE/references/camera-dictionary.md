# Camera Dictionary — Seedance 2.0 Reference

> Comprehensive camera movement, lens, and modifier reference for AI video prompt engineering.
> Chinese terms included where they boost Seedance recognition.

## Basic Movements (Level 1)

| English Term | Chinese (CN) | Description | When to Use | Example Prompt Snippet |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Pan Left | 向左平移 | Camera rotates horizontally left on fixed axis | Reveal environment, follow lateral motion | `camera pans left to reveal the cityscape` |
| Pan Right | 向右平移 | Camera rotates horizontally right on fixed axis | Reveal environment, follow lateral motion | `slow pan right across the desert horizon` |
| Tilt Up | 向上倾斜 | Camera rotates vertically upward on fixed axis | Reveal height, show power, awe | `camera tilts up along the skyscraper facade` |
| Tilt Down | 向下倾斜 | Camera rotates vertically downward on fixed axis | Reveal ground detail, show vulnerability | `camera tilts down to the character's hands` |
| Zoom In | 变焦推近 | Focal length increases, subject grows in frame | Emphasize detail, increase tension | `slow zoom in on the character's eyes` |
| Zoom Out | 变焦拉远 | Focal length decreases, subject shrinks in frame | Reveal context, show isolation | `zoom out to reveal the vast landscape` |
| Dolly In | 推镜头 | Entire camera moves physically toward subject | Intimacy, focus, entering a space | `dolly in toward the subject's face` |
| Dolly Out | 拉镜头 | Entire camera moves physically away from subject | Detachment, reveal, farewell | `dolly out as the character stands alone` |
| Truck Left | 左移 | Camera moves laterally left (whole rig) | Follow walking subject, reveal parallel space | `camera trucks left alongside the running figure` |
| Truck Right | 右移 | Camera moves laterally right (whole rig) | Follow walking subject, reveal parallel space | `camera trucks right through the market stalls` |
| Crane Up | 摇臂上升 | Camera rises vertically on crane/jib | Ascend from detail to wide, triumph | `crane up from the street to the rooftops` |
| Crane Down | 摇臂下降 | Camera descends vertically on crane/jib | Descend from overview to detail, grounding | `crane down into the crowded square` |
| Orbit / Arc | 环绕镜头 | Camera circles around the subject | Hero reveal, power, 360 examination | `camera orbits slowly around the statue` |
| Arc Shot | 弧形运镜 | Partial orbit, curved path around subject | Dynamic portrait, tension | `arc shot around the two characters facing off` |
| Tracking Shot | 跟踪镜头 | Camera follows a moving subject at constant distance | Follow action, journey, chase | `tracking shot following the cyclist through traffic` |
| Static Shot | 固定镜头 | Camera is completely still, no movement | Contemplation, tableau, stillness | `static shot of the empty room, golden hour light` |
| Push In | 推近 | Gentle forward movement toward subject | Build tension, draw attention | `gentle push in on the glowing object` |
| Pull Out | 拉远 | Gentle backward movement away from subject | Release tension, reveal | `slow pull out revealing the full destruction` |
| Pedestal Up | 升降台上升 | Camera moves straight up without tilting | Adjust framing vertically, reveal height | `pedestal up to eye level with the giant` |
| Pedestal Down | 升降台下降 | Camera moves straight down without tilting | Lower perspective, reveal ground | `pedestal down to the child's eye level` |

| Fast / Rapid | Energetic, urgent pace | `rapid tracking shot through the corridor` |

| Gradual | Progressive acceleration or deceleration | `gradual crane up from the field to the sky` |

| Decelerating | Speed decreases through the shot | `decelerating orbit settling behind the subject` |

## Lens Pollution Effects (Secret Weapon)

These "imperfections" dramatically increase perceived realism and cinematic quality in AI-generated video.

| Effect | Chinese | Description | Realism Boost | Example Prompt Snippet |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Lens Flare | 镜头光晕 | Light streaks from bright source hitting lens | High | `lens flare from the setting sun bleeding across the frame` |
| Fog on Lens | 镜头起雾 | Condensation misting the glass | Very High | `fog on the camera lens gradually clearing to reveal the scene` |
| Water Droplets | 镜头水珠 | Rain or splash on lens surface | Very High | `water droplets on the lens, rain-soaked street scene` |
| Lens Distortion | 镜头畸变 | Barrel or pincushion warping at edges | Medium | `slight barrel distortion from the wide-angle lens` |
| Chromatic Aberration | 色差 | Color fringing at high-contrast edges | Medium | `subtle chromatic aberration on the edges, vintage lens look` |
| Light Leak | 漏光 | Warm light bleeding into frame edges | High | `warm light leak on the left edge, analog film feel` |
| Dust on Lens | 镜头灰尘 | Visible dust motes on glass | Medium | `dust particles visible on the lens in the shaft of light` |
| Vignetting | 暗角 | Darkened corners of frame | Medium | `natural vignetting, dark corners framing the subject` |
| Anamorphic Flare | 变形宽银幕光晕 | Horizontal streak flare unique to anamorphic lenses | Very High | `anamorphic lens flare, blue horizontal streak across the frame` |
| Breath on Lens | 哈气 | Brief fogging from exhalation near camera | Very High | `breath fogs the lens momentarily in the cold air` |
| Fingerprint Smudge | 指纹 | Slight oily smudge softening part of frame | Medium | `faint smudge on the lens corner, gritty authenticity` |
| Rain Streak | 雨痕 | Running water trails across lens surface | High | `rain streaks running down the lens surface during the storm` |

```

[LIGHTING CONDITION],
```

## Seedance-Specific Tips

1. **Mixed EN/CN boosts accuracy.** Use English for the scene description and sprinkle Chinese camera terms for movements: `镜头缓慢环绕 the ancient temple, 逆光 golden hour`
2. **Lens pollution = instant realism.** Adding one imperfection (fog, flare, droplets) makes AI video dramatically more believable.
3. **Specify speed explicitly.** "Slow" is ambiguous. "Very slow 3-second pan" is precise.
4. **Layer modifiers.** Combine emotion + speed + style: `cinematic slow steadicam` outperforms single keywords.
5. **Name the lens.** "85mm f/1.4" triggers more specific visual characteristics than just "shallow DOF."
6. **Aspect ratio shapes mood.** 2.39:1 automatically triggers a more cinematic render. 9:16 optimizes for vertical.
7. **Start with the shot size.** Seedance parses the opening words of the prompt heavily. Lead with `Close-up` or `Wide shot` for reliable framing.
8. **Combine at most 2-3 movements.** More than three simultaneous camera instructions cause conflict and reduce quality.
